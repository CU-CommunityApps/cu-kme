Kuali Mobility Enterprise launched by Indiana University, Cornell University, University of Cambridge

Posted: Bob Flynn
Date: July 6, 2011

Kuali FoundationThe Kuali Foundation has announced the launch of Kuali Mobility Enterprise, a collaboration to develop a next-generation toolset for connecting mobile devices to information systems that are fundamental in teaching, learning, administration, and campus life. The software will provide a sustainable infrastructure for meeting the growing demand for tools and applications on mobile devices, that can be accessed any time and anywhere. The collaboration is the eighth Kuali project.
